About SalamaNet AI

Problem Statement

Increased cyber threats in public & education institutions due to poor cyber hygiene.
Phishing attacks targeting teachers, students, and government officials are becoming rampant.
There’s a lack of real-time detection and response in most Kenyan systems.

Our Solution

SalamaNet AI is a network-facing, AI-driven model that detects threats, anomalies, and phishing attacks in real time.
Our system uses ML to scan suspicious emails, sites, and attachments, sending instant alerts to administrators and automatically quarantining or blocking potential threats.
